* with auto 100%, on the background-image, 
  it will keep the aspect-ratio. background-size: auto 100%; /*width heigth */
* but when we keep the background-image: 100% auto, the image will be cropped, 
  and we can also control the crop.
* cover is a setting, that ensures it will always fill the container.
* even if the image size is small, it will zoom in.
* an alternative is contain. it will ensure it to not to crop the image.
* with contain, the image will not ensure that the container is filled,
  but it ensures that the entire image is visible, unlike the cover.
