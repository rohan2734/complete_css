* combine values of multiple properties in a single property(the shorthand property)
border-width: 2px;
border-style:dashed | solid;
border-color:orange;

*shorthand property
border: 2px dashed orange

*if some of the properties are not mentioned in the shorthand, then the default would be chosen
* also the order doest matter in the shorthand 


margin-top: 5px;
margin-right:10px;
margin-bottom:5px;
margin-left:10px;

*shorthand:
margin: 5px 10px 5px 10px;
margin: 5px 10px; top&bottom  left&right

margin:10px //applied to all the sides
